# Calora Design System (DESIGN.md)

> Calora is a calorie tracker where the camera is the input. Warm like a café, clean like a kitchen, calm like the moment after a satisfying meal.

See `/DESIGN.md` at the project root for the full token spec and per-screen designs. Quick reference:

- **Single accent**: warm coral `#ff6f4d` — NOT fitness green
- **Display typeface**: Cal Sans (Inter fallback), never italic
- **Button shapes**: 20px radius pills with colored shadow on primary
- **Three macro colors**: protein=#7c6cf0 lavender, carbs=#f59e0b amber, fat=#ec4899 pink
- **No emoji in UI text**. ✓ and 🔥 are exceptions as standalone indicators
- **Empty states** lead with action, not apology
- **Animations**: 240ms max, no bounce, no spinner >3s without text

When unsure, prefer the calmer/more restrained option.
